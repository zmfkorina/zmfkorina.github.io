F.A.Q.

It really depends on the size and the complexity of the projects. Also how expensive the materials are. Wool and Cotton are more expensive than Acrilic for example. If it's something small like a headband or a keychain, I charge around 5 euro per product. If it's something bigger, like a blouse, we should discuss the design together and see what materials you want used, what colors and based on that and the difficulty of the design I can estimate a price.

If I have a tutorial for it, I really can make you anything. It might take a while for me to figure out the pattern and gather the materials I need, but I am confident I can do it with a bit of patience. Until now I've made amigurumi, clothes like hats and blouses and tiny accessories like earrings or coasters. If you don't have a tutorial for your desired product, no worries, message me what you desire and we will figure it out together.

This one also depends on multiple factors. The complexity and size of your order for once, how many other orders I have to complete as I am a one person team, how busy I am with my school and job and how difficult it is to gather the materials. Based on how urgent it is for you to have me complete your order, we can figure it out, so don't hesitate to message me!

Unfortunately, I wasn't lucky enough to have someone teach me so I started by watching Youtube tutorials. You first need to get the basics in. I recommend Bella Coco's Absolute beginner series if you've never held a crochet hook before. After you get the basic stitches in, try some simple projects like headbands. I ordered the tools I have from Aliexpress. This is the set I have, but really, you only need one hook and some yarn. Try bulkier yarn and bright colors, as it makes it easier to see what you're doing. Most important is to have fun and be patient. I was super slow at first!
